Features

Stop writing firmware.
Start building agents.

OpenCastor decouples the brain from the body. Define your robot in YAML, pick your AI, and let the framework handle everything else.

Swap AI Brains Instantly

Switch between Gemini, GPT-4.1, Claude, Hugging Face, and Ollama with a single line change in your config. Zero code rewrites. Your robot behavior stays the same — only the brain changes.

Hardware Agnostic

Built-in drivers for PCA9685, Dynamixel, Serial, and GPIO. From $50 kits to $50K arms.

5-Minute Setup

Interactive wizard with presets for popular Amazon robot kits. One command to install, one to run.

Safety First

Multi-layered safety kernel: anti-subversion defense, work authorization for destructive actions, physical bounds enforcement, tamper-evident audit logs, and real-time safety telemetry. Emergency stop in every loop.

RCAN Standard

Open spec for robot configs. Portable, validated, ecosystem-ready. Your YAML works everywhere.

Live Dashboard

Web UI with voice control, chat, and live camera feed. Works on any screen — phone, tablet, or HDMI display.

How It Works

Three steps to intelligent.

No PhD required. No firmware. No custom drivers. Just config and go.

01

Choose Your AI

Pick from Gemini, Claude, GPT-4.1, Hugging Face (1M+ models), or run locally with Ollama. Switch anytime — one line in your config.

02

Define Your Robot

Describe your hardware in a simple YAML file. Use a community preset or build your own from scratch.

03

Deploy

One command to install, one to run. Your robot is now an AI-powered agent. Send it commands via WhatsApp, voice, or chat.

The Magic

Infinite possibilities.
